Ragu

Description:

Ragu is the perfect combination of a delicious tomato sauce seasoned and flavored with meats, garlic, and onions. Perfect for a bowl of home made pasta.

This recipe can be simply modified into a classic bolognese to compliment a traditional lasagna.

Ingredients:

  • 1lb Ground beef (80/20)
  • 1lb Mild Italian sausage
  • 1 large white onion
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 1 can of tomato paste (8oz)
  • 1 to 2 cups of red wine (your preference, cheap wine works fine)
  • 2 28oz cans of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can of dice tomatoes
  • 3 tablespoons of italian seasoning (or to taste)
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. In a dutch oven brown the sausage and ground beef together over medium high heat. Once the meat is browned use a slotted spoon or strainer to remove the meat and set aside.
  2. Fill the bottom of the pan with olive oil and turn heat to medium-low, add the onion and brown until caramelized. You'll want to let this cook for about 15 minutes to get a good caramelization.
  3. Add the garlic and cook for 1 to 2 minutes.
  4. Make a well in the center of the pan, add the can of tomato paste and slowly mix into the onions and garlic (slightly frying the tomato paste).
  5. Add the red wine, important to cover all of the onion mixture and have some room to reduce (you'll get the feel for it after making a few batches).
  6. Let reduce by half (enjoy a glass of the red wine while you wait!).
  7. Once the wine is reduced add the canned tomatoes, meat that was set aside, and italian seasoning.
  8. Let stew for 30 minutes to a half an hour. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  9. Cook your favorite pasta and serve (the best pasta for ragu is wider noodles such as Pappardelle or Rigatoni).

Bolognese Variation:

If you want to make a bolognese. Simply add 2 carrots and 2 celery ribs, place them in the food processor with the onion and finely mince them, then cook them at the same time you'd cool the onion.
